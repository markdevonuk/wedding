Accommodation

Accommodation is available on-site for guests who have requested this on their returned RSVP. The rooms in the lodge are shared and therefore we are unable to provide private rooms however each room has its own en-suite facilities. The ceremony and reception venue are just a few steps away across the courtyard.

If you have requested a room on-site for the weekend, please contact either the bride or groom for details on making payments.
